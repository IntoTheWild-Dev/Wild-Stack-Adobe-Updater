# For the Boss — Adobe Exchange Setup (Step by Step)

## What you need before starting

- [ ] Your Adobe ID (the one you use for Creative Cloud — if you don't have one, create free at https://account.adobe.com)
- [ ] Company name + website URL
- [ ] A square logo/icon (512x512 pixels PNG)
- [ ] 3-5 screenshots of each plugin in action
- [ ] A short description for each plugin (1-2 sentences)

## Step 1: Join Adobe Exchange as a Developer

1. Go to https://exchange.adobe.com
2. Click "Sign In" (top right) → log in with your Adobe ID
3. Click your profile icon → "Developer Console" or go to https://developer.adobe.com/console
4. Accept the developer terms
5. Fill in your company profile: business name, website, contact info
6. That's it — you're now a registered developer

## Step 2: We Prepare Your Plugins

Your developer will give you 3 files:
- `WildStackAECompSwap.zxp` (After Effects)
- `WildStackIDAssetSwap.ccx` (InDesign)
- `WildStackPSAssetSwap.ccx` (Photoshop)

Plus the icon and screenshots you prepared.

## Step 3: Submit Each Plugin

For EACH of the 3 plugins, repeat these steps:

1. Go to https://exchange.adobe.com → "Submit a Product"
2. Choose "Creative Cloud plugin"
3. Fill in:
   - **Product name**: (see names below)
   - **Description**: (the description you prepared)
   - **Category**: Workflow & Automation
   - **Pricing**: Free (with in-app licensing handled by our updater)
4. Upload the plugin file (.zxp or .ccx)
5. Upload icon (512x512)
6. Upload screenshots (at least 3)
7. Select compatible app: "After Effects 2024+" (or InDesign, Photoshop)
8. Submit for review

### Plugin Names for Exchange
| Plugin | Exchange Listing Name |
|--------|----------------------|
| After Effects | **Wild Stack AE Comp Swap** |
| InDesign | **Wild Stack Asset Swap** |
| Photoshop | **Wild Stack Asset Swap** |

## Step 4: Wait for Review

- Adobe reviews take 3-5 business days
- They may ask for changes — your developer can help
- Once approved, the plugins are live on the marketplace

## That's It

4 steps. Whole process takes about 1 hour of your time plus review wait.
